# PRODUCT.md

## Purpose

BTR is a distributor management system focused on sales execution, inventory control, and receivable collection.

The system helps distribution companies manage daily operational activities from sales transaction creation through inventory movement and customer payment collection.

BTR is designed to support operational users rather than accounting or ERP users.

---

# Product Vision

Provide a practical and reliable operational system for distribution businesses.

The system prioritizes:

* Fast transaction processing
* Accurate inventory tracking
* Effective receivable monitoring
* Support for field sales operations

---

# Target Users

## Sales Administration

Responsible for sales transaction processing and customer validation.

## Sales Personnel

Responsible for customer visits, order collection, invoice delivery, and payment collection.

## Warehouse Staff

Responsible for picking and packing goods.

## Inventory Administration

Responsible for inventory monitoring, stock adjustment, and return processing.

## Purchasing Administration

Responsible for purchase recording and stock replenishment.

## Finance Administration

Responsible for receivable management, payment recording, and tax reporting.

---

# Core Business Capabilities

BTR provides capabilities for:

* Sales Management
* Inventory Management
* Purchasing Management
* Receivable Management
* Customer Return Management
* Warehouse Fulfillment
* Tax Reporting
* Mobile Sales Synchronization

---

# Product Scope

The primary focus of BTR is operational distribution management.

The system manages:

* Customers
* Products
* Sales Transactions
* Inventory
* Receivables
* Returns
* Purchases

The system is not intended to be a full ERP or accounting platform.

---

# Success Criteria

BTR is considered successful when users can:

* Create sales transactions efficiently
* Maintain accurate inventory records
* Monitor customer receivables
* Process customer returns
* Support warehouse fulfillment activities
* Collect and record customer payments
* Produce operational and regulatory reports

---

# Product Principles

## Operational First

Operational activities should be simple and efficient for daily users.

## Business Reality Over Ideal Process

The system should support actual business operations, including practical exceptions and operational constraints.

## Single Source of Truth

Business data is owned and maintained by the main BTR system.

External systems synchronize from BTR rather than becoming independent sources of truth.

## Incremental Evolution

The product evolves according to business needs and operational realities while preserving existing business continuity.

---

# Out of Scope

The following areas are outside the primary responsibility of BTR:

* General Ledger
* Financial Accounting
* Human Resource Management
* Manufacturing
* Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
* Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

These capabilities may integrate with BTR but are not core product objectives.
