# ADR-002: Historical Master Data Assumptions

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-06-25  
**Milestone:** M32.B1  
**Deciders:** Solution Architect, Business Owner (confirmed)

---

## Context

Historical replay reconstructs monthly KPIs from **transactional tables** (Faktur, Invoice, Stok Mutasi, Piutang, …) joined to **master tables** (Customer, Brg, Supplier, SalesPerson, Kategori, Wilayah).

BTR master tables store **current state only**. They do not version:

- Item → Supplier (Principal) assignment
- Item → Kategori (category)
- Customer → Wilayah
- Customer plafond
- Item HPP (cost)

Without historical master versioning, replay must adopt explicit assumptions or introduce a large master-data migration project.

---

## Decision

### 1. Item → Supplier (Principal) is immutable for the replay window

For the **36-month retention period**, historical replay resolves Supplier/Principal for Item KPIs and Item→Supplier relationships using **current** `BTR_Brg.SupplierId`.

**Do not** introduce historical master versioning for this backfill.

### 2. Other master dimensions use current state (documented)

| Dimension | Replay behavior | Acceptable inaccuracy |
| --------- | --------------- | --------------------- |
| Item category (`KategoriId`) | Current value | Peer group (Radar) may differ from historical reality |
| Customer Wilayah | Current value | Radar peer comparison approximate |
| Customer Klasifikasi | Current value | Segment breakdown approximate |
| Item HPP | Current `Hpp` | Inventory valuation trends approximate |
| Customer plafond | Current `Plafond` | Historical plafond breach signals approximate |
| Salesman Wilayah | Current value | Radar peer group approximate |

### 3. Transactional history is authoritative for facts

Sales omzet, purchase spend, stock movement quantities, and payment events are derived from **dated transactional rows** filtered to each replay month. Transaction void semantics (`VoidDate = '3000-01-01'`) match live aggregator rules.

### 4. Point-in-time reconstruction for balances

Open piutang and stock position KPIs use **AsOf month-end** reconstruction from Piutang/Lunas and Stok/Mutasi history — not current master snapshot fields like `BTR_Customer.CreditBalance`.

### 5. Aggregator rules are current, not time-travelled

Backfilled months use **today's** aggregator formulas and KPI catalog definitions. Historical periods do not replay deprecated business rules that may have applied years ago.

---

## Consequences

### Positive

- Avoids multi-year master versioning project — largest scope reducer.
- Aligns with business owner confirmation for Item→Supplier.
- Consistent with how live workers already attach current master dimensions at refresh time.

### Negative

- Historical Radar peer groups and principal attribution may differ from what management experienced at the time — must be communicated.
- Piutang and stock AsOf reconstruction is computationally expensive and may have edge-case inaccuracy.

### Mitigation

- Document limitations in operator runbook and profile help text (future).
- Product Owner sign-off on piutang AsOf policy before Customer backfill.
- Reconciliation tests against Desktop reports for sample entities/months.

---

## Alternatives Considered

| Alternative | Rejected because |
| ----------- | ---------------- |
| Historical master versioning (SCD Type 2) | Out of scope; large master-data redesign |
| Skip Supplier/Item relationship history | Reduces profile value; owner confirmed Supplier immutability for items |
| Use only transactional dimensions on documents | Sales documents carry salesman; not all dimensions present on all doc types |

---

## Compliance

Satisfies approved constraint **§3 Historical Master Data Assumption**.
